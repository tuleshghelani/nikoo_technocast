Our Materials & Capabilities

At Nikoo Technocast, we specialize in two primary types of iron castings:

Gray Iron (C.I) Castings

Excellent vibration damping properties with superior wear resistance and thermal conductivity, ideal for:

  • Engine blocks and cylinder heads
  • Brake components and housing
  • Machinery bases and frames
  • Industrial equipment components

Ductile Iron (S.G) Castings

Combines the castability of gray iron with the strength and ductility of steel, perfect for:

  • Safety-critical automotive components
  • Pressure-containing parts
  • Structural components requiring high tensile strength
  • Heavy machinery applications

Production Capabilities

  • Weight Range: 0.5 kg to 120 kg
  • Dimensional Tolerance: CT6-CT8
  • Surface Finish: 125-250 RMS
  • Material Grades: GI-200 to GI-400, SGI-400 to SGI-700
